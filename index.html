The Hard Truth: Brushing Isn't Enough ğŸª¥

Even with perfect brushing, you miss about 30-40% of tooth surfaces! Here's what builds up:

  • Plaque: Sticky film of bacteria (you can brush this off)
  • Tartar/Calculus: Hardened plaque (you CANNOT brush this off!)

Once plaque hardens into tartar, only professional tools can remove it. That's where we come in!

What Happens During Professional Cleaning? ğŸ”§

Step 1: Examination (5 mins)
We check for cavities, gum disease, and oral cancer signs. Prevention is better than cure!

Step 2: Scaling (15-20 mins)
Using ultrasonic scalers, we remove tartar from above and below the gum line. You'll hear buzzing - that's normal! It might feel slightly uncomfortable but NOT painful.

Step 3: Polishing (10 mins)
We use a gritty paste to polish your teeth smooth. This removes surface stains and makes it harder for plaque to stick.

Step 4: Fluoride Treatment (5 mins)
Optional but recommended! Strengthens enamel and protects against cavities.
